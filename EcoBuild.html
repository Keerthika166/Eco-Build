About EcoBuild

Champions of Conscious Construction

We believe every building has the potential to give back to the planet. Our team of certified green architects, engineers, and sustainability advisors work together to make that vision a reality for clients across India and beyond.

Our Mission
Accelerate the transition to sustainable construction by making green building accessible, affordable, and aspirational for every stakeholder.
Our Vision
A world where every structure built contributes positively to its ecosystem, community, and the global climate agenda.
Our Values
Integrity in every assessment, innovation in every design, and impact measured in real environmental outcomes.

Comprehensive Green Building Services

Green Building Design
Biophilic, energy-passive architectural designs that harmonize with nature. We integrate daylighting, natural ventilation, and living walls from the ground up.

Energy Audit
Comprehensive on-site and remote energy audits that identify inefficiencies, quantify losses, and prioritize improvements with measurable ROI timelines.

Solar Panel Consultation
Site assessment, system sizing, vendor selection, and financial modeling for solar installations across residential, commercial, and industrial projects.

Water Conservation Planning

Waste Management Solutions
